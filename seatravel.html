SOUTH
EAST ASIA

We went on a boat ride on the river and got to see a lot of mountains, restaurants, and birds. We got lucky and even saw monkeys. During this section of our trip we were on a cruise, sailing along Halong bay. We went to a village where I bought some jewelry and went to a fish foot spa, it was so much fun!
I went to Vietnam with my family. We stayed in Hanoi then moved towards the rivers. My mom and I got our nails done and ate well! We went to Hidden Gem Cafe Hanoi and had some egg coffee, it was really good. We ended the trip seeing the Hanoi train that goes through Tran Phu and Phung Hung streets in the Old Quarter. It was kinda scary!
We went to Thailand and saw the Calypso Cabaret. I had so much fun as they played songs like LaLisa and Born This Way. I loved walking around and exploring the beaches there. I felt so comfortable and had a lot of amazing food like pad kra pao moo saap, a minced pork with rice and egg dish.
I did a tour of some of the temples in Thailand. I saw a lot of Buddha statues, including this one in the picture and a reclining Buddha. It was the largest statue I've ever seen, I loved exploring all of the temples. I even went to the Terminal 21 mall, which has an airport theme with different country attractions.
I went to Singapore as well and got to see the Gardens by the Bay. Every night they host a light-show and I got to see it with my family. I saw this place in Crazy Rich Asians and seeing it in person felt like a cool moment.
I went to Merlion Park to see the Merlion statue. We did some shopping in the afternoon. At night we went to Lau Pa Sat to try hawker food. I loved learning about the history of Singapore and being able to see other cool landmarks.
