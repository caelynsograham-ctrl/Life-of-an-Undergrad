CHINA

I studied at Beijing Normal Univerity for 2 weeks to get a certificite in manderin. One of the first things we did was walk the Great Wall of china. My legs were killing me. Overall I was really proud of myself for making it up. I bought an overpriced tea set as a gift for my mom.
I dressed in a Ming Dynasty hanfu. The owners of the shop were so nice, they made me feel beautiful. I was nervous at first but I came prepared with my personal makeup and the people there were very nice. The lady doing my hair took so much time and care with me and gave me so many hair accesories. SO many people took pictures of/with me that day.
The University took us to a lot of temples. This is the Temple of Heaven. I loved every place we went. I took so many pictures started to get really close with the other students that I went with. We were able to go to the Lama Temple, and I was able to pray and light incense.
